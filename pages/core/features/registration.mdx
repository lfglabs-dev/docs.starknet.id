# Domain Registration

## Why Domain Registration?

The Starknet ID application enables users to register STARK domain names within the Starknet network. Once registered, these STARK domains become usable across all [applications](/core/integrations) that integrate Starknet ID.

## How to register your domain

1/ Navigate to the [Starknet ID application](https://app.Starknet.id/)

2/ Connect your wallet

![img](public/illustrations/connect-wallet.webp)

3/ Choose your STARK domain

![img](public/illustrations/choose-domain.webp)

4/ Complete your registration

![img](public/illustrations/complete-info.webp)

Now, users can pay in ETH or STRK.

![img](public/illustrations/pay-strk.webp)

5/ Navigate to the [Starknet ID ecosystem](/core/integrations).
